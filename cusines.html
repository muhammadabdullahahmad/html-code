Famous Foods of Lahore, Pakistan

nihari

A slow-cooked meat stew, usually made with beef or mutton, and served with naan. It's a breakfast favorite in Lahore.

paaye

A traditional dish made from the trotters (feet) of cows or goats, cooked with spices in a thick gravy. It's often enjoyed with naan or paratha.

haleem

A thick, savory dish made from wheat, barley, lentils, and slow-cooked meat, garnished with fried onions, lemon, and ginger.
