Chicken Tikka Pizza

A very flavorful pizza with chicken tikka and an Indian flavored pizza sauce. This homemade pizza sauce has so much more flavor than the usual sauce, especially the Indian flavors which I love. Chicken Fajita Pizza is a very spicy and flavorful pizza with the Mexican flavors. It has salsa, chicken fajita mix, and lots of cheese baked on a crispy crust which is cooked on a stovetop skillet.
